ABOUT US

Why choose us?

Wraith architects has been creating top quality designs for past 10 years. Our

Wraith architects is an designing based firm based out of Chennai.
We re-imagines your need to create a visually stunning yet highly
efficient spaces. We are multifaceted professional designing firm

Institutional. Every home or space we design reflects the perfect
balance of luxury and designer appeal with comfort.
